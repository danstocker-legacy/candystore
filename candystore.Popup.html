Class: Popup

candystore. Popup

The Popup trait allows widgets to be opened and closed like popups. Popups maintain parent-children relationship with the widget that created them, but they are rendered right under the body element. It is vital therefore that whatever happens inside the popup must trigger widget events since they are the only way to notify the parent widget of changes. Popups may be closed by clicking outside of the widget's DOM. Expects to be added to Widget classes.
